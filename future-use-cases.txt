# Future Modifications & Expansion Ideas

This AI automation workflow was intentionally designed to be modular and expandable.

The current implementation focuses on:

* Amazon product sourcing
* Pinterest affiliate content generation
* SEO optimization
* Automated workflow execution

However, the same architecture can be adapted for many additional use cases.
